# NIST CSF Risk Assessment: Healthcare Clinic

## 📋 Project Overview
I performed a comprehensive risk assessment for a fictional medical clinic (**Riverview Family Clinic**) using the **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0**. This project demonstrates the ability to bridge the gap between technical vulnerabilities and business impact in a regulated healthcare environment.

## 🛠️ Key Skills Demonstrated
* **Frameworks:** NIST CSF 2.0, HIPAA Regulatory Compliance.
* **Risk Management:** Asset Identification, Threat Modeling, Qualitative Risk Analysis ($Likelihood \times Impact$).
* **Security Controls:** Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), Full Disk Encryption (BitLocker).
* **Business Logic:** Disaster Recovery Planning, Business Continuity (BCP), and Executive Reporting.

## 📂 Project Structure
* **NIST_Risk_Assessment_Report.pdf**: The final 3-page executive report including the Audit Findings and Remediation Roadmap.
* **Risk_Matrix_Artifact.xlsx**: The master risk register containing inherent and residual risk calculations.
* **Screenshots/**: Visual evidence of the color-coded risk heat map and conditional formatting logic.

## 🚀 Summary of Impact
Identified a critical vulnerability in the clinic's billing portal (Risk Score: 9/9). Proposed a remediation strategy that reduced the overall residual risk by **70%** through technical controls and administrative policy changes.
